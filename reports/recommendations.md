# Recommendation Framework

---

## 1. Objective

To improve sprint delivery predictability by addressing planning inefficiencies, reducing spillover, and stabilizing delivery capacity.

---

## 2. Strategic Recommendations

### 2.1 Reduce Sprint Commitment

Action:

- Reduce planned work by ~60–70%

Rationale:
Align workload with observed capacity (3 items per sprint)

---

### 2.2 Implement Capacity-Based Planning

Action:

- Use historical velocity as planning baseline

Rationale:
Prevents overcommitment and improves reliability

---

### 2.3 Introduce Mid-Sprint Monitoring

Action:

- Conduct progress reviews during sprint

Rationale:
Early identification of blockers reduces spillover

---

### 2.4 Improve Work Distribution

Action:

- Balance workload across team members

Rationale:
Prevents bottlenecks and improves throughput

---

### 2.5 Establish Risk Monitoring

Action:

- Track commitment reliability and spillover in real-time

Rationale:
Enables early risk detection and mitigation

---

## 3. Expected Outcomes

- Increase reliability above 70%
- Reduce spillover across sprints
- Stabilize sprint velocity
- Improve delivery predictability

---

## 4. Implementation Priority

| Priority | Action                         |
| -------- | ------------------------------ |
| High     | Reduce sprint commitment       |
| High     | Capacity-based planning        |
| Medium   | Mid-sprint tracking            |
| Medium   | Work distribution optimization |
| Low      | Advanced risk monitoring       |
