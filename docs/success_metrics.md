# Success Metrics & KPIs

## Delivery Performance Metrics
* **Commitment Reliability**: (Completed Story Points / Committed Story Points) * 100
* **Spillover Rate**: (Unfinished Story Points / Total Planned Points) * 100
* **Velocity Stability**: Percentage variance in completed points across rolling 3-sprint cycles.

## Predictive Risk Indicators
* **Mid-Sprint Risk Score**: Weighted calculation based on remaining hours vs. remaining days.
* **WIP Ratio**: (Items In-Progress / Team Capacity) to detect bottlenecks early.

## Project Success Criteria
* **Quantifiable Goal 1**: Reduce aggregate sprint spillover rate by **15%** within 3 cycles.
* **Quantifiable Goal 2**: Achieve and maintain **Commitment Reliability > 80%**.
* **Quantifiable Goal 3**: Enable system-driven risk detection by **Day 3** of a standard 10-day sprint cycle.
