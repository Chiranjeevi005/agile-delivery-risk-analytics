
# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project: Agile Delivery Risk Analytics

---

## 1. Business Problem

Current sprint execution shows severe inefficiencies in delivery:

- Only ~25% of planned work is completed within sprint
- High levels of spillover (unfinished work carried forward)
- Unstable and declining sprint velocity
- Lack of data-driven planning decisions

This results in:

- Missed delivery timelines
- Poor predictability
- Increased operational risk

---

## 2. Business Objective

To improve sprint delivery predictability by:

- Aligning planned work with actual team capacity
- Reducing spillover across sprints
- Providing visibility into delivery risk
- Enabling data-driven sprint planning decisions

---

## 3. Stakeholders

| Stakeholder      | Role                                           |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Product Owner    | Defines priorities and scope                   |
| Scrum Master     | Ensures sprint execution and process adherence |
| Development Team | Executes sprint work                           |
| Business Analyst | Analyzes performance and defines improvements  |
| Project Manager  | Tracks delivery and risk                       |

---

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI                    | Definition                                      |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Commitment Reliability | Completed work ÷ Planned work                  |
| Spillover Rate         | Unfinished work ÷ Planned work                 |
| Sprint Velocity        | Work completed within sprint                    |
| Delivery Risk Level    | Classification based on reliability + spillover |

---

## 5. Success Criteria

- Increase commitment reliability above 70%
- Reduce spillover across consecutive sprints
- Stabilize sprint velocity trends
- Enable proactive risk identification before sprint completion

---

## 6. Business Impact

- Improved delivery predictability
- Better sprint planning accuracy
- Reduced operational inefficiencies
- Increased stakeholder confidence
