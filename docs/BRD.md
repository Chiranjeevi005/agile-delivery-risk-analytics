# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Overview
The Agile Sprint Performance & Delivery Risk Analyzer aims to provide early warning signals for sprint delivery risks by analyzing historical and real-time Jira data.

## Business Objectives
* Identify key risk indicators (KRIs) for sprint failure.
* Automate risk detection mid-sprint to allow for corrective action.
* Standardize reporting across multiple agile teams.
