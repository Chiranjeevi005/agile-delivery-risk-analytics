
# Assumptions and Constraints

---

## 1. Assumptions

- Sprint duration is consistent across analysis
- All issues are properly tracked and recorded
- Completed_In_Sprint accurately reflects delivery
- Data used is representative of actual sprint behavior
- Team structure remains stable across sprints

---

## 2. Constraints

- Limited number of sprints analyzed (small dataset)
- No real-time integration with Jira (simulated data)
- No individual-level performance tracking
- External dependencies not modeled
- Risk model is rule-based, not predictive ML

---

## 3. Limitations

- Insights may not generalize across all teams
- Small dataset limits trend reliability
- Risk classification is heuristic, not probabilistic

---

## 4. Future Enhancements

- Integrate real-time Jira data
- Expand dataset across multiple teams
- Apply predictive modeling for risk forecasting
- Add team-level and individual-level analysis
